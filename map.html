Where is the affordable housing?

The Federal Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD) considers monthly rent under 30% of a person's income to be affordable.

Often, this prohibits lower-income persons from living near areas of greater economic opportunity.

So, where is all the affordable housing?

How to use:

  1. Determine affordable monthly rent amount with Calculator below.
  2. Use map menu to choose number of bedrooms needed.
  3. Drill down into price ranges to see which locations are available.
  4. Click on a location to see its data.

Dollar value labels on map are hourly wage needed to afford selected bedroom level. (Default: 1BR)
